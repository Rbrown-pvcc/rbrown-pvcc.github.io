Chapter 2: Quick Definitions

  1. natural language: regular talk ‚Äî messy for code.
  2. pseudocode: plain-English steps that read like code.
  3. sequential operation: do step 1 ‚Üí 2 ‚Üí 3, no branches.
  4. input: what you feed the algorithm.
  5. output: what it gives back.
  6. conditional statement: only runs if the check true.
  7. boolean condition: true/false test.
